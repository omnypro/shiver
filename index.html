The perfect Twitch companion for your Mac. Never miss your favorite streamers again.

Available in the Mac App Store for $2.99 Current Version v1.0 — Mountain Lion (10.8) Only

Stay connected to your favorite gamers

Twitch connects gamers around the world by allowing them to broadcast, watch and chat from everywhere they game. Shiver lives in your menu bar and lets you know how many of your favorite streamers are currently broadcasting.

As long as you have a Twitch account and a couple of favorite streamers, Shiver will make sure you never miss a second of epic gaming.

  • Lives in your menu bar and stays out of your way, showing a count next to the icon if you'd like.
  • Utilizes Mountain Lion's Notification Center to let you know when streams go live and gives you one-click access to them.
  • Configurable refresh times put you in control of when you recieve notifications, or turn them off alotgether.
